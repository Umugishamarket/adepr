Ibyerekeye ADEPR

ADEPR ni itorero ryashinzwe mu 1940 rigamije kugeza ubutumwa bwiza bwa Yesu Kristo kubari mu Rwanda hose. Rifite abakristo basaga miliyoni 5 mu gihugu hose, rifite amashuri arenga 150, amavuriro 37 n’ibigo byinshi bifasha iterambere rusange.

Rikoresha uburyo bugezweho mu gutanga serivisi ku bakristo n’abayobozi barwo bagera mu nzego zose kuva ku rwego rw’itorero kugera ku rwego rw’igihugu.

Serivisi Zitangwa

  • Kwiyandikisha nk’umukristo mushya
  • Gusaba icyemezo cy’imyitwarire cyangwa ubunyamuryango
  • Gusaba transfert hagati y’insengero
  • Gusaba inkunga (mu rwego rw’itorero, paroisse cyangwa ururembo)
  • Gusaba gusengerwa / inama / amahugurwa

Kanda hano usabe serivisi

Amashuri ya ADEPR

ADEPR ifite amashuri y'incuke, abanza, ayisumbuye ndetse na kaminuza. Amashuri yose akorera ku mahame y'uburezi bufite ireme n'indangagaciro z'iyobokamana. Amashuri ya ADEPR abarizwa mu ntara zose z'u Rwanda.

  • Amashuri y'incuke: 48
  • Amashuri abanza: 57
  • Amashuri yisumbuye: 35
  • Kaminuza: Pentecostal University of Rwanda (PUR)

Ibigo Nderabuzima n’Amavuriro

Itorero ADEPR rifite uruhare rukomeye mu rwego rw’ubuzima binyuze mu mavuriro n’ibigo nderabuzima ritanga hirya no hino mu gihugu:

  • Amavuriro yita ku barwayi: 12
  • Ibigo Nderabuzima: 25
  • Serivisi z'ubujyanama ku buzima bwo mu mutwe n’imibereho

Insengero Ziri Hafi Yawe

Ushobora kureba insengero zose zemewe za ADEPR mu gihugu hose hakoreshejwe ikarita y’ubutaka. Serivisi izatuma umenya aho wasengera hafi yawe aho waba uri hose mu Rwanda.
