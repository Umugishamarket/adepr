Ibyerekeye ADEPR

ADEPR (Association des Eglises de Pentecôte au Rwanda) ni Itorero rya Pentekote ryatangiriye mu Rwanda kuva mu 1940. Rifite insengero hirya no hino mu gihugu, amashuri, amavuriro n’imishinga y’iterambere. Intego ni ugutanga ubuzima bwiza bw’umwuka n’umubiri ku Banyarwanda bose.

  • Abakristo: Barenga miliyoni 2 mu gihugu hose
  • Abari mu mijyi: Basaga 600,000
  • Abari mu byaro: Barenga 1,400,000
  • Amashuri: 316 (ayisumbuye, abanza n'incuke)
  • Amavuriro: 21 (harimo ibigo nderabuzima n’amavuriro y'ibanze)
  • Ishuri rikuru: Pentecostal University of Rwanda
  • Abakozi b’itorero: Abapasitori n’abadiyakoni barenga 3,000

Serivisi z’abakristo

Umukristo wese ashobora gusaba izi serivisi binyuze kuri uru rubuga:

  • Gusaba icyemezo cy'imyitwarire (Recommendation letter)
  • Gusaba icyemezo cy'ubunyamuryango
  • Kwimukira ku rundi rusengero (gusaba transfert)
  • Gusaba gusengerwa cyangwa kuganirizwa n’abakozi b’Imana
  • Kwiyandikisha mu matsinda (korali, abasenzi, abajyanama...)
  • Gusaba inkunga mu bihe bikomeye
  • Kwiyandikisha nk’umukristo mushya
